I am a writer, researcher, and editor who specializes in issues of social and economic justice, equity, and feminism. My work has appeared or is forthcoming in Room, The Fiddlehead, Policy Options, Corporate Knights, Rotman Management, and several other publications. I also have a breadth of experience in communications and effective knowledge mobilization.

I am currently a Research Associate at the University of the Toronto, where I co-authored the COVID-19 policy plan A Feminist Economic Recovery Plan for Canada: Making the Economy Work for Everyone. I earned an MSc from the London School of Economics and an honours BA from McGill University.
