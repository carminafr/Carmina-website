I am a writer, researcher, and editor. My work has appeared in Grain, Room, The Fiddlehead, Policy Options, Corporate Knights, Rotman Management, and several other publications. I have a breadth of experience in communications and effective knowledge mobilization.

I am currently a Senior Research Associate at the University of the Toronto, where I co-authored the COVID-19 policy plan A Feminist Economic Recovery Plan for Canada: Making the Economy Work for Everyone. I am also the co-host and producer of the podcast Busted, which debunks myths that hamper the progress of equality. I earned an MSc from the London School of Economics and an honours BA from McGill University.
